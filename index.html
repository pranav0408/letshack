About the event

MSHacks series now named as Let's Hack is one of the biggest events that is conducted by MSTC LPU. The sole aim of this hackathon is to provide a competitive environment where students to make something innovative.

Hacking is building things that you always wanted to have but no one has built it yet. It's to come up with an amazing idea and work tirelessly on it. It is to fail, fail again and fail better. Try out new things and learn while doing that. It's to work together, collaborate and build things that are innovative. It is to be a better programmer. With that spirit, Microsoft Technical Community LPU is conducting a hackathon - Let's Hack 3.0 and invites all developers and hackathon enthusiasts to participate in an interesting and engaging hackathon.

5+ Speakers & 10+ mentors

We are bringing very talented men and women from various parts of country to make this hackathon one of the happening not only in terms of arrangement but also on terms of the quality of content.

36 hrs Hackathon

For this 36 hours you will be working on various problem statements and issues. You will be trying to make your best solution and we will take care of your refreshments and enjoyment.

Per round Elimination

Elimination will be based on per round basis. There will be nearly 3 rounds of evaluation on 12,24,36 hours from start respectively.

Filled with opportunities

Get a chance to meet leadership experts of now and get a chance to talk to them and take your career to next level.

Diversity at Heart



At MSHACKS, we create an environment that brings developers together irrespective of their gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. Ever since its inception, MSHACKS has been extremely devoted to the cause of celebrating diversity among its participants and organisers. Over the years, we have strived to make sure that women get a chance to showcase their experience with all fairness.
In MSHACKS 2.0, the best performing girls’ team will receive special prizes and swags from our side as a token of appreciation for their remarkable efforts in leu of increasing girl participation. In this year we even have organisations and NGOs supporting us in this mission.

FAQ

1Who should attend Let's Hack 3.0?

Whether you are new to the open source and Hackathon world or an experienced developer, you will learn something new at Let's Hack. The Convergence of developers is designed in the form to help build communities and build strategies to strengthen their whole ecosystem. Being a Let's Hack attendee we will be bringing to you a great chance to interact and build connections alongside. This whole event is designed to educate new developers about the Developement and Corporate world and help existing developers strengthen their outreach.

2 Will there be prizes?

Of course there will be prizes although it isn’t all about winning but what you learn and build during those 36 hours. But don’t worry there will be a panel of judges that will select the three best hacks based on creativity, technical difficulty, design, and productiveness. Prizes for first, second and third place will be given. Sponsors also offer prizes for specific criteria, which they judge themselves.

3What is included in the Hackathon registration?

Hackathon registration gives you a chance to get selected to attend the Hackathon. After the registrations close, the selected participants will be informed through email highlighting and our Hackathon Partner platform in the further steps. There will be lunch and refreshments provided.

4 Can I get a confirmation of my Let's Hack registration?

Upon completing the online registration process, if you're selected, you will be emailed a confirmation email with details from Let's Hack. If you are already selected (as on the Hackathon portal) and can’t see this email, please check your spam folder.

5 Will the slides and presentations be shared after the event?

Yes they will be made available on our webpage and communicated to you in the ‘Thank You’ email after the event. Please note that the slides will only be provided with the discretion of the presenting organisations.

6 How should I behave at Let's Hack?

Be respectful and considerate of your fellow hackers. We're all here to learn and have a good time. Everyone deserves the chance to hack free of the fear of being harrased. See the Let's Hack Guidelines for more details.

7 Any unanswered questions?

If there is something that is still not clear or we didn’t cover please contact us at admin@microsoftlpu.com or at instagram or facebook.

8How can I apply?

You can go to our Hackerearth portal where all the information is available about the application procedure.

9When will the application open?

Applications open at 30th May 2019

10 When will the application close?

Applications close at 1st Aug 2018

11 What should I bring to Let's Hack?

Please bring a valid ID (any Photo ID), your hackathon ticket, a computer (preferably a laptop), chargers, and any hardware you will use for your hack. You can also bring a sleeping mattress, pillow, toiletries etc. Food will be provided at the event. No firearms, weapons, alcohol, or illegal drugs are allowed on event.

12 What size are the teams? What happens if I don’t have a team?

The teams are a maximum of 4 people. You can start off the event already with a team or you can meet with people at the event who also don’t have a team and form here

13 Will there be travel reimbursements?

There may be some travel reimbursements for outstanding candidates for sure, we will communicate the details with the selected attendees. The details for the same will be communited through email.

14 How much does it cost?

It's absolutely free! There is no entry fee, Nada. Let's Hack will provide you with plenty of food, snacks, swag, and prizes throughout the 36 hours.
